A little about me...

Those who know me, define Lucia as a smiling, kind woman, who is also loaded with funny thoughts. I feel comfortable organising and planning visits, trips and events of any kind, and above all I love working in groups where communication prevails.

I have been raised surrounded by art and science, and those have become my passion in many ways: music, cinema, illustration, technology and culture. My empathy helps me to really transmit my ideas to other people and to get a better understanding of the world around me.
